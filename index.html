SEU Researchers

SEU Researchers is an android app that provides various research-related information to the students of Southeast University including all the available research supervisor and their area of research interest. It helps the students to send research proposal to the supervisors and reach them easily like never before. The system is super dynamic and user-friendly.

Technology Stack: Native Android (JAVA), Firebase

Website: iqbalriiaz.github.io/seu-researchers

GitHub: github.com/iqbalriiaz/SEU-Researchers-APP

Features
  • A student will be able to easily group up with other students online.
  • Students will be able to
    • see which supervisors are available,
    • see faculties research requirements and their area of interest,
    • find whether they meet their supervisor's requirements or not.
  • Teachers will be able to track research groups under their supervision.
  • Create a virtual communication system between teachers and students by allow students sending research proposal and get is accepted or rejected by the supervisors.
  • Overall a dynamic research management system.

Arduino Robo Car

The Arduino Robo Car Application is able to remotely control your devices with Bluetooth Module and Arduino Board. The app allows you to control an Arduino-based car or robot or anything you made that has a Bluetooth module attached.

Native Android: Flutter (JAVA)

Features
  • Simple remote control interface.
  • Robot mode (continue data send when the button is pressed and hold)
  • Car mode (single data send on button press)
Usage
  • Home Automation System.
  • Car and motor controlling.
  • Light controlling
  • LEDs Controlling etc.
